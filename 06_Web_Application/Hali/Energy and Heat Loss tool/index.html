Help me choose my property's age

The age of your home gives us an idea of the construction methods and building materials used. You can find the year that your property was built on your Energy Performance Certificate (EPC) report, if you have one.

If you live in England or Wales you can find your EPC here. If you live in Scotland you can find your EPC here.

If you don't have an EPC, you could find the details on your house deeds. If you own your home, your survey or home report may tell you the age of the building.
